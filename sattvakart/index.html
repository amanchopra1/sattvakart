Why Sattva Kart and Sona Moti Wheat

A small, soil-connected initiative to bring back pure, desi grains and honest food.

Sattva Kart was started with a simple question: what if families could access the same pure grain that farmers keep for their own homes, instead of the polished, mixed and over-processed wheat that reaches most markets.

We grow and source Sona Moti wheat in Haryana with a focus on natural methods – without pesticides and with minimal external inputs. The grain is then stone-ground on chakki to preserve fibre, micronutrients and flavour.

Every batch is handled in small lots so that you get fresh, aromatic and clean food that supports sattvic living, better digestion and long-term health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Sattva Kart based?

We are based in Haryana, with farming and storage operations around Fatehabad. Grain is cleaned and stone-ground locally before being shipped to you.

Is Sona Moti wheat really different from regular wheat?

Sona Moti is a desi wheat variety known for its rich aroma, softness and better digestibility compared to many modern hybrids. It is often preferred by people following traditional, sattvic or health-focused diets.

Do you use pesticides or chemical fertilisers?

Our focus is on natural, low-chemical farming. We avoid pesticide spraying and do not chemically treat seed or flour. The idea is to keep food as close to its original form as possible.

How fresh is the flour when it reaches me?

Flour is milled in small batches. We generally grind and dispatch within a short window so that rotis made from Sattva Kart atta have the aroma and texture of freshly milled wheat.

Do you ship across India?

Yes, we can arrange shipping to most major cities and towns in India. WhatsApp us your pin code and we will confirm delivery options and approximate transit time.
